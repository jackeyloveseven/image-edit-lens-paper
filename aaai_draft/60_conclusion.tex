\section{Conclusion}\label{sec:conclusion}

Across the tested editors, early probes predict realized edits; in the
Qwen lineage, an image-like code is translated to velocity at L52--54.
Lenses and interventions bound readability, sufficiency, and
carrier-bounded writability, while held-out selection and CFG truncation
demonstrate practical use. We do not claim irreversible commitment,
necessity, or architecture-wide universality.
